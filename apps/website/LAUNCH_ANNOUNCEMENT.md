# WISE² Website Launch Announcement

**🚀 We're Live!**

The new WISE² marketing website is officially live at **https://wise2.net**

---

## What's New

### Completely Redesigned Website

We've rebuilt the WISE² website from the ground up with a modern, premium aesthetic that reflects our brand evolution.

**What you'll see:**

#### 1. Glass-Inspired Design
A sleek, modern visual style with subtle glass effects and transparency layers. The design feels premium and futuristic while remaining fully functional across devices. Our signature neon green accents pop against a deep black background, creating a high-contrast, sophisticated look.

#### 2. Interactive Project Gallery
Browse our 6 completed projects in an interactive gallery. Each project showcases our expertise across different domains with high-quality previews and descriptions. Filter by category to find projects that match your interests.

#### 3. Brand Refresh
Updated color system, refined typography, and consistent visual language throughout. Everything is designed to feel cohesive, modern, and aligned with WISE² 's strategic vision.

#### 4. Responsive & Fast
Optimized for all devices—mobile phones, tablets, and desktops. The site loads quickly and feels responsive to every interaction.

---

## Key Features

### For Visitors

- **Easy Navigation:** Intuitive header navigation with mobile-friendly hamburger menu
- **Clear Services:** Browse what we offer in clean, scannable cards
- **Meet Our Teams:** Explore our different "Worlds" (business units)
- **Intake Form:** Simple way to start a project with us—just fill out the form
- **Professional Footer:** Links to all company info and social media

### For the Business

- **Portfolio Showcase:** Display our best work and expertise
- **Lead Generation:** Intake form captures new project inquiries
- **Brand Consistency:** Every section reflects our design system
- **Performance:** Optimized for search engines and page speed
- **Maintainability:** Built on modern tech (Next.js + Tailwind CSS)

## Technical Details

**Built with:**
- Next.js 14 (modern React framework)
- Tailwind CSS (utility-first styling)
- Framer Motion (smooth animations)
- WISE² Design System (centralized components)

**Performance targets:**
- Lighthouse Score: 90+
- First load: <1.5 seconds
- Fully responsive on all devices

**Updated deployment process:**
- Manual SSH deployment (reliable and transparent)
- Zero downtime updates
- Automated health checks
