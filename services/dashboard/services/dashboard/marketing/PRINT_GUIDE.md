## 🖨️ Printing Instructions

### For Digital Printing (Xerox, Canon, etc.)
1. Open `flyer_phase1.html` in Chrome or Firefox
2. Press `Ctrl+P` (Windows) or `Cmd+P` (Mac)
3. Set to "Save as PDF" first
4. In PDF printer settings:
   - Paper size: Letter (8.5" × 11")
   - Scaling: 100% (no auto-fit)
   - Margins: 0.25" all sides
5. Save PDF
6. Send to local print shop or office printer

### For Professional Offset Printing
1. Export to PDF (as above)
2. Convert PDF to CMYK using Affinity Publisher or Canva
3. Add crop marks (0.125" from edge)
4. Specify 300 DPI minimum
5. Provide to printing company with these specs:
   - Trim size: 8.5" × 11"
   - Bleed: 0.125"
   - Color mode: CMYK
   - Stock: Glossy 100 lb

### For Online Print Services (Vistaprint, FedEx Print, etc.)
1. Export as PDF at 300 DPI
2. Upload to service's design tool
3. Select "Flyers" category
4. Paper type: Glossy or Matte (100 lb)
5. Quantity: 250-1000
6. Add bleed (service usually handles this)
7. Proof before finalizing order

## Brand Consistency

### Color Guidelines
- Primary Blue: Use for headlines, emphasis
- Purple: Use for secondary elements, accents
- Green: Reserved for checkmarks, positive indicators
- Dark: Use for high-contrast text areas

### Typography
- Headlines: Bold, high contrast
- Body text: Regular weight, readable at small sizes
- Metrics: Largest text, immediate impact
- Footer: White text on dark background for contrast

### Layout Principles
- Scanning-friendly (top to bottom)
- Metrics immediately visible
- Features clear and scannable
- CTA strong and visible
