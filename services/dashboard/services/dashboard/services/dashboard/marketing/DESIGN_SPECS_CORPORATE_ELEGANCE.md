## ANIMATION & TRANSITIONS

### Master Schedule
```
Title → Problem:       Fade 0.6s (professional)
Problem → Solution:    Dissolve 0.8s (elegant)
Solution boxes:        Staggered 0.3s apart
Metrics → Badges:      Slide up 0.6s
Badges → CTA:          Fade 0.8s
Total timing:          20s exactly
```

### Easing
- Fade transitions: Linear (professional feel)
- Scale animations: Ease-in-out (subtle, not distracting)
- Counter animations: Linear (reliable appearance)

## BRAND GUARDRAILS

✅ DO:
- Use Georgia serif for headlines (authority)
- Keep gold accents subtle (5-10% of screen)
- Maintain professional distance (no casual language)
- Use warm, office-appropriate lighting
- Emphasize reliability & compliance

❌ DON'T:
- Use bright colors (stay professional)
- Rush animations (keep pace measured)
- Show casual/playful elements
- Use modern sans-serif for headlines
- Emphasize speed/startup culture vibes
