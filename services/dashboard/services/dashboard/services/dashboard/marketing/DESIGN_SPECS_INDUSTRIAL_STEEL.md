## ANIMATION & TRANSITIONS

### Master Schedule
```
Title → Problem:       Hard cut (0.2s, technical)
Problem → Solution:    Wipe effect (0.6s, industrial)
Solution specs:        Pop-in staggered 0.2s apart
Metrics → CTA:         Fade 0.7s
Total timing:          20s exactly
```

### Effects
- No soft fades (except CTA)
- Sharp edges, minimal blur
- "Glitch" effect on metric numbers (optional, technical aesthetic)
- Metal texture overlays (subtle, 5% opacity)

## BRAND GUARDRAILS

✅ DO:
- Use monospace fonts (technical precision)
- Emphasize numbers & metrics (engineering credibility)
- Keep colors industrial (grays, steel, warning orange)
- Use sharp, clean lines (no soft transitions)
- Reference technical specifications

❌ DON'T:
- Use gradients/soft effects (too smooth for brand)
- Show casual development scenes
- Use playful language or tone
- Emphasize "startup" vibes
- Include human emotion/lifestyle elements
