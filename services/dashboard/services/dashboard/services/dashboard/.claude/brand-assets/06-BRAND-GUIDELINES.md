## VISUAL IDENTITY

### Logo

**Primary Logo:** The wordmark "WISE² CORE" in a modern geometric sans-serif. The superscript "²" is integral to the mark.

**Logo Characteristics:**
- Geometric, constructed (not hand-drawn)
- Modern sans-serif (Inter or similar)
- Tight letter spacing
- Works at all scales (16px favicon to billboard)

**Logo Variations:**
1. Horizontal (full wordmark, primary)
2. Stacked (for square formats)
3. Icon mark (the "²" symbol alone, for profiles)
4. Reversed (white on dark backgrounds)

**Clear Space Rule**
Minimum clear space = the height of the letter "O" in the mark.

**Minimum Size**
- Digital: 64px width
- Print: 1 inch width

**Never:**
- Stretch, compress, or distort
- Add drop shadows, gradients, or 3D effects
- Outline or add borders
- Place on busy backgrounds without a container
- Change colors outside approved palette
- Replace "²" with regular "2"

### Typography

**Primary Typeface: Inter**
Modern, geometric sans-serif. Neutral, readable, trustworthy. Works at all sizes.

**Secondary Typeface: JetBrains Mono**
For code and technical content. Readable, distinctive, developer-native.

**Type Hierarchy**

| Level | Font | Weight | Size | Usage |
|-------|------|--------|------|-------|
| **Hero Headline (H1)** | Inter | Bold (700) | 48–64px | Page headlines, hero sections |
| **Section Header (H2)** | Inter | Semibold (600) | 28–36px | Section titles |
| **Subheader (H3)** | Inter | Semibold (600) | 18–24px | Subsection titles |
| **Body Copy** | Inter | Regular (400) | 16px | Paragraphs, explanatory text |
| **Small Text** | Inter | Regular (400) | 14px | Metadata, captions |
| **Code/Technical** | JetBrains Mono | Regular (400) | 12–14px | Code blocks, commands |

**Typography Rules**
- Sentence case for headlines (not title case)
- Line height minimum 1.5 for body copy
- No all-caps in body copy
- Use contractions ("you'll" not "you will")
- Spell out 0–9; use numerals for 10+

### Design Principles

**1. Clarity Over Decoration**
Every visual element serves a purpose. Prioritize information hierarchy and readability. A well-designed dashboard is beautiful because it's clear.

**2. Consistency at Scale**
Identity works at favicon size to billboard. Every element (logo, color, type, icons) feels cohesive at any scale. Detail matters.

**3. Honest and Grounded**
No gradients, blur effects, or trendy 3D. Feel substantial and real. Surfaces flat. Colors solid. Typography clear.

**4. Functional Beauty**
Beauty comes from functionality. Well-spaced interface = beautiful. Clear hierarchy = beautiful. Consistent alignment = beautiful.

## VERBAL IDENTITY

### Brand Voice

**Voice Essence**
Wise² Core sounds like a trusted peer builder—honest, practical, grounded in execution, and unwilling to waste anyone's time.

**Tone Dimensions**

**Casual ←——→ Formal**
Casual side, but structured. Use contractions. Write like you talk. Respect infrastructure's seriousness.
Example: "You'll want to set up monitoring before you go live." ✓

**Calm ←——→ Energetic**
Calm with underlying urgency. Pace is steady but purposeful. Emphasize through word choice, not punctuation.
Example: "Deploy in 10 minutes. Then monitor it properly." ✓

**Serious ←——→ Witty**
Serious with occasional dry humor. Humor earned, not forced.
Example: "PostgreSQL doesn't have 'good enough' settings. It has the settings that matter and the ones that tank your database at 2am." ✓

**Accessible ←——→ Authoritative**
Authoritative but never condescending. Assume intelligence; don't assume knowledge.
Example: "Your database needs replication—that's a second copy kept in sync so if the primary dies, traffic switches over without downtime." ✓

---

### Voice Qualities

**Honest** — Tell the truth, even when inconvenient. Name tradeoffs. Trust through accuracy.
- Do: "This works well for teams up to 50. Beyond that, you'll outgrow the single-database model."
- Don't: "Scales infinitely to any size."

**Practical** — Every message helps someone do something. Useful, specific, actionable.
- Do: "Here's exactly how to set up SSL. Copy this command, run it, then add this line to your config."
- Don't: "Wise² Core provides robust security infrastructure through industry-leading cryptographic protocols."

**Direct** — Say what you mean. No hedging, no corporate-speak. Respect time.
- Do: "Your database is down. Check your connection pool size. [Link to docs]"
- Don't: "We're experiencing some service disruption."

**Grounded** — Reference real use cases, real builders, real constraints.
- Do: "Priya runs a ₹150K/month SaaS on Wise² Core. Here's her setup."
- Don't: "Suitable for businesses of all sizes seeking scalable solutions."

**Ambitious** — Believe in founders. Help them see how far they can go.
- Do: "You're going to grow beyond this. Here's how to scale without migrating platforms."
- Don't: "A nice solution for small operations."

**Transparent** — Show how you make decisions. Share tradeoffs. Demystify.
- Do: "We chose PostgreSQL because [specific reasons]. Alternatives like MongoDB would [tradeoff]."
- Don't: "Powered by cutting-edge database technology."

### Vocabulary

**Words We Use:**
Deploy, build, ship, execute, launch, production-ready, battle-tested, proven, affordable, sustainable, bootstrap, founder, builder, ambitious, grounded, practical, honest, scaling, growth, ownership, control, speed, reliability, transparent, documented, clear.

**Words We Avoid:**
Revolutionary, game-changing, world-class, synergy, leverage, optimize, seamless, intuitive, effortless, cutting-edge, state-of-the-art, enterprise, solution, disruptive, empower, unlock, robust, scalable (without specificity), ecosystem.

**Brand-Specific Language:**
- Users = "founders" or "you" (never "users")
- Deploy = consistent term (not "launch," "activate")
- Infrastructure = standard term we use
- Wise² Core = full name on first mention, then "Wise²"

---

### Writing Style Rules

**Sentence length:** Short by default. Mix in medium sentences. Long sentences rare.

**Punctuation:** Em-dashes encouraged. Exclamation marks rare. Question marks powerful.

**Capitalization:** Sentence case (not title case). Proper nouns capitalized.

**Numbers:** Spell out 0–9. Use numerals for 10+. Always include units ("10 minutes").

**Contractions:** Use them. "You'll," not "you will."

**Active voice:** Default. "We built this" not "this was built."

**Humor:** Earned only. No jokes for jokes' sake. Dry observations on-brand.

---

### Things We Never Say

- "Revolutionary," "game-changing," "cutting-edge," "world-class" (hollow hype)
- "Empower you to unlock the potential of..." (corporate motivation language)
- "Seamlessly," "effortlessly," "intuitively" (lazy adjectives)
- "Enterprise-grade scalability" (too vague)
- "Trusted by thousands of businesses" (meaningless without specifics)
- "24/7 support" or "always available" (absolutes we can't guarantee)
- "We believe in..." (corporate philosophy nobody cares about)
- Exclamation mark spam (we're focused, not excited)

## BRAND IN USE

### Digital

**Website:**
- Hero: Dark navy or white background, bold white/navy headline, orange CTA
- Supporting sections: Alternating white/light gray backgrounds
- High contrast typography, founder/dashboard imagery
- Clear value proposition above fold

**Dashboard/App:**
- Light backgrounds (white/#F9FAFB)
- Dark navy for primary UI (headers, navigation)
- Orange for primary CTAs
- Gray for supporting information
- Monospace for code
- Generous spacing (8px baseline grid)

**Email:**
- Header: Logo + navy line
- Body: White background, navy headers, Inter typography
- CTAs: Orange button with white text
- Footer: Navy background, white text, clear unsubscribe

**Social Media:**
- Profile picture: Logo (squared up, dark background)
- Bio: Benefit-focused, clear, no jargon
- Post style: Mix of product screenshots, founder stories, educational snippets, community highlights
- Cadence: 3–5 posts/week

---

### Print

**Business Cards:**
- Front: Logo (left), minimal contact info, white background
- Inter Semibold for name, Regular for details
- No decorative elements
- Substantial cardstock

**Letterhead:**
- Header: Logo (left corner) + company info
- Navy line (2px) below header
- White body
- Margins: 1" all sides

**Presentations:**
- Slide template: White or navy backgrounds
- Headlines: Bold Inter, 48px+, navy or orange
- Body: Regular Inter, 16px+, dark gray
- Orange accent bars for emphasis
- Real screenshots (not mockups)
