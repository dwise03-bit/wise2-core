# WISE² CORE — D2C MARKETING SYSTEM

*Direct-to-Founder Go-to-Market Strategy*

---

## 01 — UNIT ECONOMICS BASELINE

**Revenue Model:**
- **Freemium tier**: Free deployment (open source, self-hosted)
- **Managed hosting tier**: $20–100/month (hands-off operations)
- **Enterprise tier**: Custom pricing (dedicated support, compliance)

**Target CAC & LTV:**
- **CAC target**: $0–50 (acquired through content, community, word-of-mouth)
- **AOV (first year)**: $240 (managed hosting at $20/month average)
- **LTV (3-year)**: $1,200+ (assuming 50% annual retention, expansion to enterprise)
- **LTV:CAC ratio**: Target 10:1+ (infrastructure products should have very high LTV:CAC because of stickiness)
- **Payback period**: <6 months from first dollar of managed hosting

**Unit Economics by Customer Segment:**

| Segment | Year 1 Revenue | Retention | Year 3 LTV | CAC | LTV:CAC |
|---------|---|---|---|---|---|
| Bootstrap founder (self-hosted) | $0 | N/A | $0 | $0 | ∞ |
| Growth-stage founder (managed) | $240–480/yr | 70% | $500–1,000 | $20–50 | 10–20:1 |
| SMB co. (managed + support) | $1,200–2,400/yr | 85% | $3,000–5,000 | $100–200 | 15–25:1 |
| Enterprise (custom tier) | $5,000–20,000/yr | 90% | $20,000–60,000 | $500–2,000 | 10–30:1 |

## 03 — OWNED CHANNEL STRATEGY (Email + Community)

**Email Capture Mechanisms (in priority order):**
1. **Free tier signup** — anyone who wants to try Wise² Core enters the email funnel
2. **Documentation signup** — "Get email guides on [topic]"
3. **Newsletter popup** — "Deploy tips + case studies, 1x/week"
4. **Onboarding series** — automated after signup

**Core Email Flows to Build (priority order):**

1. **Welcome series (3 emails, days 1/3/7):**
   - Email 1: Welcome + 10-minute quick start
   - Email 2: Success story from similar founder
   - Email 3: "Here's what happens next" (expansion path to managed hosting)

2. **Onboarding drip (5 emails, days 1–14):**
   - Deploy your first service
   - Set up monitoring
   - Add backups
   - Invite your team
   - Deploy to production

3. **Managed hosting nurture (lifecycle):**
   - "Thinking about managed hosting?" at day 30
   - Success story at day 60
   - Social proof at day 90

4. **Activation (when they hit milestones):**
   - First deployment successful
   - First alert triggered
   - 10 deployments reached

5. **Win-back (lapsed users, monthly):**
   - Outreach at 30/60/90 days inactive

6. **Newsletter (1x/week or 2x/month):**
   - Deployment tips
   - Case studies
   - New features
   - Community highlights

**Community Strategy (Discord/Forum):**
- **#introductions** — new members introduce themselves
- **#deployments** — infrastructure questions and help
- **#showcase** — founders share their setups
- **#wins** — celebrate milestones
- **#feedback** — feature requests
- **#announcements** — updates and roadmap

## 04 — RETENTION & EXPANSION STRATEGY

**Free tier retention:**
- Weekly email tips
- Monthly case studies
- Product updates
- Community wins

**Managed hosting expansion:**
The natural progression:
- Free tier (self-hosted): Founder gets infrastructure running
- Managed tier ($20–100/mo): Founder outsources operations
- Enterprise (custom): Company needs compliance and support

**Expansion triggers:**
- After first successful deployment
- At ~10 deployments (ops burden becomes real)
- When founder mentions uptime/backup concerns

## 09 — PAID ACQUISITION (When to Use It)

Don't start with paid. Start with organic. But when ready:

**Google Ads (Search):**
- Keywords: "affordable infrastructure", "production platform for startups"
- Budget: $500–1,000/month after organic traction proven
- ROAS target: 3–5x

**Influencer/Partner sponsorships:**
- Sponsor 3–5 founder podcasts or newsletters
- Budget: $2,000–5,000/month

**Retargeting:**
- Website visitors who didn't sign up
- Budget: $300–500/month

## 10 — GROWTH PHASES & SHIFTS

**Phase 1 (Months 1–3): Validation**
- Goal: Prove product-market fit
- Focus: Organic, community, early case studies
- Success metric: 100+ users, 5+ paid signups

**Phase 2 (Months 3–6): Authority**
- Goal: Establish thought leadership
- Focus: Content, community growth, case studies
- Success metric: 500+ users, 50+ paid signups, <$40 CAC

**Phase 3 (Months 6–12): Scale**
- Goal: Expand to enterprise
- Focus: Enterprise sales, partnerships, paid ads
- Success metric: 2K+ users, 300+ paid, <$30 CAC

## SUMMARY

Wise² Core's D2C marketing is built on one principle: **Build trust through content, community, and transparency. Let the product sell itself.**

The playbook:
1. **Acquire** through organic content and community
2. **Activate** through obsessive onboarding
3. **Retain** through weekly value delivery
4. **Expand** through managed hosting upsell
5. **Advocate** by celebrating founder wins
