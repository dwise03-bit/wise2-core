## ANIMATION & TRANSITIONS

### Master Schedule
```
Title → Problem:       Elegant dissolve (0.7s)
Problem → Solution:    Fade + rise (0.8s, sophisticated)
Pillars:              Staggered fade-in 0.4s apart
Pillars → Proof:      Slide transition (0.6s)
Proof → CTA:          Elegant fade (0.8s)
Total timing:         20s exactly
```

### Easing Philosophy
- All transitions: Ease-in-out (smooth, refined)
- No sharp cuts (too harsh for luxury brand)
- Timing slow & deliberate (luxury = patience)
- Subtle scale effects (never dramatic)

## BRAND GUARDRAILS

✅ DO:
- Use serif fonts (timeless elegance)
- Emphasize uncompromising standards (luxury positioning)
- Use gold accents sparingly but meaningfully
- Maintain white space (sophistication)
- Use slow, deliberate animations (never rushed)
- Reference "distinction" and "excellence"

❌ DON'T:
- Use bright colors or saturation (too bold)
- Show startup/casual culture
- Use sans-serif for headlines (breaks luxury aesthetic)
- Rush animations (luxury = patience)
- Emphasize price or cost (luxury is assumed expensive)
- Use playful or modern language
